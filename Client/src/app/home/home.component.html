OUR ESTEEMED HISTORY

Since 2014, Swift Gas has been distributing LP Gas since 2014, and delivering gas cylinders to both the private and the commercial sector. From the start, we have been distributing bulk LP Gas with full support and backup from our producing companies. Now we have our own delivery trucks and also supply, install and maintain bulk storage facilities. We stock a large selection of world renowned gas products, including stoves, fireplaces, heaters, braais and water heaters. Our technical staff are registered with the LP Gas Safety Association as well as the South African Gas Association (SAGA). Our staff are qualified and licensed gas technicians for domestic, commercial and industrial gas installations. This means you are guaranteed to get the quality results you need, whether its installation, servicing, or maintenance of all major brands of LP Gas appliances. We also feature a “full- for-empty” LP Gas exchange system for brands such as Easigas, Afrox, Oryx, total, and Wassa. We also do refills for Cadac for 3kg, 5kg, 7kg, and 9kg.
